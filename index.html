Cuc Phuong National Park

As the first national park of Vietnam, Cuc Phuong is one of the jewels of forest ecology. This place is not only special about green vegetation that has been nurtured and developed over thousands of years, but also is home to hundreds of rare animal species… It is the biodiversity that has helped Cuc Phuong be honored. Named as the leading national park in Asia. The typical forest type of Cuc Phuong is a tropical rain forest with green trees all year round. According to a recent survey, the national park currently has 2,234 species of higher plants and mosses. Many species are recorded within the Red Book of Vietnam.

One of the must-go sections of Cuc Phuong National Park is Caveman’s Cavern (Dong Nguoi Xua), home to the prehistoric men and their tomb as well. Located in a gigantic limestone cave, this is also a place where plenty of bat breeds have been inhabiting for centuries. Opens up to whoever sets foot in the cave is the wide interior filled with a cool breeze. The whole interior leads out to a cave’s entrance situated on a mountain peak, which is just a fine location for the wind to enter the entrance, flow back into the cave and make the entire interior filled with comfortable, cool ambiance, unlike other caverns that gradually raise their temperature as long as there are people staying in there.

The next location that should be everyone’s next destination when coming to this National Park is the Animal’s Cave (Hang Con Moong). The cave was discovered in 1974 and recognized as Viet Nam’s National Historic Site in 2007. The destination holds various evidence of the Stone Age, the process of development from the old-day, hunter-gatherer lifestyle to primitive farming.

It can be said that The Silver Cloud peak is one of the most beautiful places in Cuc Phuong Ninh Binh forest, Especially for those who want to explore and take beautiful landscape photos. To reach the top of the Silver Cloud, you will have to climb on the mountain, Although the road is a bit difficult, once you are standing above you will admire the beautiful mountainous and watery scenery. At about 2000ft, we can keep our eyes wide Cuc Phuong National Park sees the ancient capital Hoa Lu, with the magnificent Bai Dinh Pagoda and admires the full edge of Trang An scenic area located in the north of Ninh Binh.

Besides the diversity of the ecosystem, Cuc Phuong National Park has particularly 3 dining and entertainment areas for visitors to choose from Garden gate, Mac Lake area, and Central area. At Garden gate, there is a rental service of forest equipment, climbing bikes. About the Mac Lake area, people come to this place to rent collective accommodation, rooms. This is an area suitable for large groups, you can light a campfire, eat, drink, sing, and have cultural exchanges here. The most special architecture of the Central area, which attracts lots of tourists, is the stilt house. People are fascinated by its traditional and simple beauty which retains attraction from time to time.

. Especially coming to Cuc Phuong National Park in May is the time when butterflies in Cuc Phuong forest are at breeding season. With the number of individuals that can be up to millions, they are racing to show off their beauty in the sky like a beautiful "butterfly forest" like in a fairyland. After visiting and exploring for a round in Cuc Phuong, you're able to enjoy the special mountain snails only available here.Additionally, there are still many other attractive dishes such as grilled garden chicken, deep-fried porch with a cup of Muong rice wine...

Cuc Phuong National Park project is a tourist destination and historical relic area. This is the common home of hundreds of thousands of different species of flora and fauna, attracting thousands of visitors. This is also a rare and precious flora and fauna reserve of Vietnam, demonstrating the importance of plants to our lives and protecting endangered species of flora and fauna of Vietnam and the world.
